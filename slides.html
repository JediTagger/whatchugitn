WhatChaGitN?!

A group gift exchange coordination application.

Jesse Turner
October 6th, 2015

Project Goals:

Family members will be able to:

  • create, read, update and delete their Christmas wish list,
  • see other family members' wish lists,
  • see who has already committed to get a gift for other family members,
  • and commit to get a gift for other family members.
